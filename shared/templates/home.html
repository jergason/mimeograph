A mimeograph is a machine that produces copies from a stencil. Think a low-tech photocopier. Mimeograph the web application is a Twitter clone with a twist. Users upload photos that are converted to ASCII art and are posted to a feed.

Twitter has tweets, and Mimeograph has mimes. Users can follow other users to see their mimes show up in their feed, or go directly to other user's feeds to see all of their mimes.
